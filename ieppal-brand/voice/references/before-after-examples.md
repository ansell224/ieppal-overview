**Marketing headline**
- Weak: "Revolutionize Your IEP Workflow With AI-Powered Goal Drafting"
- Strong: "Draft a goal in the time it takes to sip your coffee."

**Landing page subhead**
- Weak: "Our innovative platform empowers educators with best-in-class tools to drive student outcomes."
- Strong: "Draft, review, and share IEP goals without bouncing between five tabs. *You* stay focused on the student."

**Product notification**
- Weak: "🎉 Awesome! Your IEP has been submitted successfully!!!"
- Strong: "Jamie's IEP is saved. You can share the draft with the team when you're ready."

**LinkedIn founder post (opening line)**
- Weak: "We're thrilled to announce a major milestone..."
- Strong: "The first time I ever demoed our software, I was a little embarrassed of what I'd built. But here's what I realized about product design..."
- Also strong: "This feedback kind of surprised me, but in a good way..."
- Also strong: "Two weeks ago, I had the chance to sit down with a special educator from an international school in Vietnam."

**LinkedIn founder post (AI framing)**
- Weak: "We're excited to introduce AI-powered features to IEP Pal."
- Strong: "Here's what we've heard: AI should help expediate manual tasks, not outright complete them, and give educators a starting place. The overarching theme was, could AI help speed up my process — not replace it. It's important to remember that this must be done at the **discretion of the educator**."

**LinkedIn founder post (product philosophy)**
- Weak: "Our goal is to save teachers time and improve efficiency."
- Strong: "I tell educators and school leaders we demo with that, if you're spending more than 15 minutes a day on iep-pal.com, we've failed you. We're about simplifying document storage, student data analytics, and IEP/goal writing, so that educators can focus more on in-person relationships with students."

**LinkedIn founder post (closing question)**
- Weak: "Sign up at iep-pal.com to learn more."
- Strong: "What would you add to the list above? What would you suggest and/or caution against?"
- Also strong: "What would you prioritize as a teacher?"

**Error state**
- Weak: "Oops! Something went wrong. Please try again later or contact support."
- Strong: "We couldn't save that draft just now. Your text is still here — try again in a moment, or email hello@ieppal.com if it keeps happening."

**Investor deck — problem slide**
- Weak: "The IEP market is fragmented and underserved."
- Strong: "A special ed teacher writes 20+ IEPs a year. The tools they're given were built for districts, not for them."

**Describing AI features**
- Weak: "IEP Pal's AI automatically generates goals based on student data."
- Strong: "IEP Pal drafts a first pass of the goal for you to edit — a starting place, not a finished product. What happens next is at the discretion of the educator."
