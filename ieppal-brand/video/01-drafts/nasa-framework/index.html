NASA Framework

A model for showing up for the people around you.

N
Notice
These are the words someone uses, their tone, their body language.
A
Acknowledge
"I can't imagine how difficult that was to hear." Help the person feel like their experiences are real and valid.
S
Share
Verbalize what you noticed. A bit of encouragement fits here too, if it's genuine.
A
Ask
Questions that help someone arrive at their own solutions. They're more likely to follow through because they suggested it.

N and A in particular require something easy to underestimate: undivided presence.
