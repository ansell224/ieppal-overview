# Carousel Outline — AI Doesn't Have To Be Scary: Terms

> **Design principle:** The caption is a light teaser. The carousel does the talking — every term gets its own slide so readers can pause, screenshot, and absorb one idea at a time.

---

## Slide 1 — Hook
**Layout:** Large title + sub text
- **Title:** AI doesn't have to be scary.
- **Sub text:** Here are 6 terms worth actually knowing.

---

## Slide 2 — Setup
**Layout:** Bold single statement (stat slide)
- **Title:** These 6 cover about 80% of what you'll encounter in the wild.

---

## Slide 3 — Term 1: LLM
**Layout:** Term label (small, above) + definition headline + body
- **Term label:** 1 / 6
- **Title:** LLM (Large Language Model)
- **Body:** The actual name for tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. A model trained on billions of words to predict what text should come next.

---

## Slide 4 — Term 2: Hallucination
**Layout:** Term label (small, above) + definition headline + body
- **Term label:** 2 / 6
- **Title:** Hallucination
- **Body:** When an AI sounds certain and is completely wrong. It's not lying — it's pattern-matching toward a plausible answer even when the facts don't exist.

---

## Slide 5 — Term 3: Prompt
**Layout:** Term label (small, above) + definition headline + body
- **Term label:** 3 / 6
- **Title:** Prompt
- **Body:** What you type into an AI. The more specific your input, the better the output. You're already doing this. You can get much better at it.

---

## Slide 6 — Term 4: Context Window
**Layout:** Term label (small, above) + definition headline + body
- **Term label:** 4 / 6
- **Title:** Context Window
- **Body:** The AI's working desk. Everything in your current conversation sits on it. Once the desk is full, older messages fall off — and the AI genuinely forgets them.

---

## Slide 7 — Term 5: Token
**Layout:** Term label (small, above) + definition headline + body
- **Term label:** 5 / 6
- **Title:** Token
- **Body:** How AI measures text. About 4 characters = 1 token. This is why AI tools have usage limits — they're counting tokens, not words.

---

## Slide 8 — Term 6: AI Agent
**Layout:** Term label (small, above) + definition headline + body
- **Term label:** 6 / 6
- **Title:** AI Agent
- **Body:** An AI that takes actions, not just answers questions. It can browse the web, run code, draft emails, and plan steps on its own. This one's coming faster than most people expect.

---

## Slide 9 — CTA
**Layout:** Soft close + follow prompt
- **Title:** Want to learn more?
- **Body:** Join me on the journey. Follow for the next post in the series: *AI Doesn't Have To Be Scary.*
- **CTA pill:** Which term surprised you most?
